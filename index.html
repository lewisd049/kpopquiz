KPOP Quiz

What Is KPOP Quiz?

KPOP Quiz is a new fun and engaging game, run by Matthew!!!

How To Play

A part of a KPOP song will play and when it stops, if you know the song name or artist, put your hand up. Then Matthew will pick people until there are no more people left to pick, and then we will move onto the next song.
